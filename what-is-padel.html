What Is Padel?

A simple guide for beginners in Ireland.

An Introduction to the Sport

Padel is a racket sport that combines elements of tennis and squash. It is usually played in doubles on a smaller enclosed court, where players can use the walls as part of the game. This makes rallies exciting and fun to watch.

Why People Enjoy It

Many people enjoy padel because it is easier to learn than some traditional racket sports. You do not need advanced technique to begin, which makes it welcoming for new players, families, and social groups.

How Padel Is Played

  • Padel is usually played in doubles.
  • The court is smaller than a tennis court.
  • The walls are used during play.
  • Players use a solid padel racket instead of a string racket.
  • The scoring system is similar to tennis.

Padel vs Tennis
