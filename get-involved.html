Get Involved

Simple steps to start playing padel in Ireland.

How to Start

  1. Learn the basic rules of the game.
  2. Look for a club, court, or beginner session near you.
  3. Borrow or buy a padel racket.
  4. Play with friends or join a doubles session.
  5. Keep practising and enjoy the social side of the sport.

What You Need

Racket

A padel racket is solid and easier for beginners to control than many people expect.

Sportswear

Comfortable clothing and runners with good grip are enough for most first sessions.

Positive Attitude

Padel is social and fun, so beginners can relax and enjoy learning as they play.
